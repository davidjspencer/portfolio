My journey to UX

Hi, I am a graphic and UX designer based in Barcelona and I thrive on the challenge of finding user insights and synthesising them into design concepts.

This also reflects my different roles such as teaching, marketing, web design and graphic design. Skills developed in my career have been invaluable in helping me execute UX practices, this also demonstrates my thirst for learning and self-improvement and my ability to adapt to new challenges.

Currently freelancing I transitioned towards UX with a masters in UX design and I am driven to make a positive difference in the world.
