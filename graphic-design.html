The Brief

The name Vesper is inspired by 2 things:

  • The Vespa scooter – colorful, impulsive, vintage, classic, quality, southern/warm image
  • Vesper Lund – the true love of James Bond (Eva Green). Classy, very smart, discreet, feminine, serious, risk taker, committed, strong woman.
